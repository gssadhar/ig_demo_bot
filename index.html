Trailing P/E Ratio
Definition: Price divided by trailing 12-month EPS.

Assessment: <15x Undervalued/Value; >30x Overvalued/Growth Premium.
Total Revenue
Trailing 12-Month Top-Line
Definition: Total income from primary business operations over 12 months.
Sector Context: Benchmarked against direct industry competitors.

Net Income
Bottom-Line Profit
Definition: Profit remaining after all operating expenses, taxes, and costs.
Sector Context: Compared against sector median net profit conversions.
Assessment: Positive earnings validate a sustainable business model.
Total Debt
Balance Sheet Liabilities
Definition: Sum of all short-term and long-term financial obligations.
Sector Context: Capital-intensive sectors naturally carry higher debt.
Assessment: High debt is risky if it exceeds liquid cash reserves.
Cash Reserves
Liquidity Cushion
Definition: Total liquid cash and marketable securities available.
Sector Context: Benchmarked against total debt and quarterly burn rate.
Assessment: High cash cushions provide safety buffers during downturns.
Return on Equity (ROE)
Definition: Net income divided by shareholder equity.
Sector Context: Broader market average sits around 12%–15%.
Assessment: >15% signals excellent management capital efficiency.
Profit Margin
Definition: Percentage of revenue turning into actual net profit.
Sector Context: Cross-industry average hovers around 8%–10%.
Assessment: >20% represents high pricing power and business quality.
20-Day SMA
Short-Term Trend
Definition: Average closing price over the last 20 trading days.
Sector Context: Tactical benchmark for short-term institutional momentum.
Assessment: Trading above indicates immediate bullish momentum.
50-Day SMA
Medium-Term Support
Definition: Average closing price over the last 50 trading days.
Sector Context: Foundational line for intermediate trend health.
Assessment: Price holding above 50 SMA confirms a healthy bull market.
